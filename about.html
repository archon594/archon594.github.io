Recommendations

New to the gaming scene? Take a look at some of these recommended tabletop games to start with.

Warhammer 40k
One of the largest, if not THE largest tabletop war game of the modern age. Head off into space at the head of the imperial guard, fighting off evil aliens and monsters for the sake of your home world! Fight as a dying race known as the Aeldari, desperately clinging on to life as space demons hunt you down across the cosmos!
Age of Sigmar
Is Fantasy a little more your thing? Age of Sigmar is a fantasy game made by the same creators as Warhammer 40k. This game is set in a world of High Fantasy, filled with magic, elves, orcs, humans, dwarves, undead and more!
Bolt-Action
Are you a history buff, or into something a bit more grounded? Look no further than Bolt-Action, a war game built around World War II. Join the side of axis, or allies in all out battles to see if you can turn the tides of history in your favor!
